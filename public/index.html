Frequently Asked Questions

Which stores do you compare?
We compare Amazon India, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, Ajio, Snapdeal, TataCliq, Nykaa, FirstCry, blinkit and Zepto.
How accurate are the prices?
We fetch listed prices from partner sites and apply caching. Prices may vary during sales.
How do price alerts work?
Set a threshold and we check prices periodically; we'll notify you when the price drops (requires signup/email service).
