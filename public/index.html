Frequently Asked Questions

Which stores do you compare?
We currently show prices from Amazon, Flipkart, Croma, TataCliQ and more. We'll keep adding sources — let us know if you want a specific store.
How accurate are the prices?
We fetch listed prices from partner sites and show delivery & coupon info when available. Prices can vary during sales; we store historical prices to provide trends.
How do price alerts work?
Set a price threshold and we'll notify you via email when the product hits that price (requires signup). Alerts are powered by scheduled background checks of product prices.
